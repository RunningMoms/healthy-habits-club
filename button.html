Café Tasting Room

Immerse yourself in the world of artisanal chocolate at Reina's Café Tasting Room. Open daily from 9 AM to 5 PM, our café offers the perfect setting to savor the finest chocolate creations.

Discover handcrafted treats made with pure Costa Rican cacao and locally sourced ingredients. Enjoy a cup of rich sipping chocolate, aromatic chocolate tea, or explore our selection of decadent chocolate bars infused with unique flavors like orange, habanero, and sea salt. Pair these delights with freshly brewed coffee or a refreshing cold beverage as you relax in our welcoming space.

  • Handcrafted chocolate bars with local flavors
  • Rich sipping chocolate and chocolate tea
  • Comfortable seating and a relaxing ambiance

Visit us today and indulge in the ultimate chocolate tasting experience. You'll leave with a newfound appreciation for the magic of cacao!

Hours: Open daily, 9 AM - 5 PM
